# Business Club Platform — Feature Overview

## The All-in-One Platform for Your Business Community

Business Club is a powerful, modern platform built for the Financial University's Entrepreneurial Club. It brings together event management, online learning, live broadcasts, AI-powered challenges, a career portal, and real-time messaging — all under one roof, available in both English and Russian.

---

## For Members

### Dashboard
Your personal command center. See upcoming events at a glance, track your active courses, and stay connected with the latest club activity. Key stats — total members, events, and courses — are always front and center.

### Events
Never miss a moment. Browse upcoming and past events, view featured highlights, and register in seconds through smart custom registration forms. Every event comes with detailed descriptions, dates, locations, and capacity information. After attending, your participation is automatically recorded.

- **AI-Powered Registration Chat** — Register for events through a natural conversation with GigaChat, Sber's AI. It guides you through the process, extracts your information, and even supports file uploads — no boring forms required.
- **Custom Registration Forms** — Each event can have its own tailored registration questions, including text fields, dropdowns, checkboxes, and file uploads.
- **Event Tickets** — Receive digital tickets with unique QR codes for seamless check-in at the door.
- **Certificates** — Attended an event? Download a professionally generated certificate of participation to showcase your involvement.

### Learning Management System (LMS)
A full-featured online education platform built right into the club.

- **Courses** — Browse available courses, read descriptions, and enroll with one click.
- **Modules & Lessons** — Each course is organized into structured modules with individual lessons that support both text content and embedded video.
- **Quizzes** — Test your knowledge with interactive quizzes tied to specific lessons.
- **Assignments** — Submit work for review by teachers and admins, with detailed feedback and grading.
- **Progress Tracking** — Watch your completion percentage grow as you work through lessons and modules.
- **Downloadable Materials** — Access supplementary files and resources attached to lessons.
- **Course Forums** — Engage in threaded discussions with fellow students and instructors within each course.
- **Lesson Comments** — Leave comments and questions directly on lessons for collaborative learning.
- **Announcements** — Stay informed with course-specific announcements from instructors.
- **Gradebook** — View your grades, rubric scores, and overall course performance in one place.

### AI Debate Challenges
Sharpen your argumentation skills in AI-powered debate battles.

- **Pick a Topic & Take a Side** — Choose from curated debate topics and argue your position against GigaChat AI.
- **Real-Time Scoring** — Your arguments are evaluated on logic, evidence, persuasiveness, and coherence.
- **Leaderboards** — Compete for top spots on per-challenge and global leaderboards.
- **Seasonal Points (FIS System)** — Earn points across a season, climb the rankings, and prove your debate mastery over time.
- **Team Championship** — Compete as part of a team for collective glory.
- **Challenge Attachments** — Admins can attach reference materials, documents, and images to challenges for deeper preparation.
- **Attempt History** — Review your past debates, scores, and AI feedback to improve.

### Video Library
Access a growing collection of educational and club-related videos.

- **Browse & Watch** — View videos with titles, upload dates, and view counts.
- **Comments** — Leave comments on videos to share thoughts and engage with the community.
- **RuTube Integration** — Videos are hosted and played through RuTube for reliable Russian-friendly streaming.

### Livestreams
Tune into live broadcasts without leaving the platform.

- **Upcoming & Live Now** — See what's scheduled and jump into active streams.
- **RuTube-Powered** — Seamless livestream playback via RuTube integration.
- **Scheduled Streams** — Know exactly when the next broadcast is happening.

### Messaging & Chat
Stay connected with your club community.

- **Direct Messages** — Send and receive private 1:1 messages with any platform member.
- **Real-Time Conversations** — Instant messaging with a clean, modern chat interface.
- **People Directory** — Search and discover other members, view their profiles, and start conversations.
- **Profile Cards** — View detailed member profiles including their role, organization, and faculty information.
- **Unread Counts** — Always know when you have new messages waiting.

### Career Portal
Discover professional opportunities through the club's job board.

- **Browse Job Openings** — View available positions with rich descriptions, requirements, and details.
- **Apply Directly** — Submit applications with your information and upload PDF resumes.
- **Candidate Account** — Create a dedicated candidate account to track all your applications in one place.
- **Application Status Tracking** — Follow your application through every stage: new, reviewing, interview, offered, rejected, or hired.
- **Recruiter Chat** — Communicate directly with recruiters about your application.

### Profile Management
Keep your information up to date.

- **Personal Details** — Update your name, patronymic, and contact information.
- **Organization Info** — Specify your organization type, name, and faculty affiliation.
- **Profile Picture** — Your avatar is displayed across the platform in chats, leaderboards, and directories.

### Platform Tour
First time on the platform? An interactive guided tour walks you through every feature, helping you get started quickly and confidently.

### Progressive Web App (PWA)
Install the platform on your phone or desktop like a native app. Full offline support, push-ready architecture, and automatic updates ensure you always have the latest experience.

### Bilingual Interface
Switch seamlessly between English and Russian. Every label, button, notification, and message is fully translated for a native experience in both languages.

### Dark & Light Mode
Choose the visual theme that works best for you. The entire platform adapts — from dashboards to forms to chat — with a polished, consistent look in both modes.

## For Admins & Head Admins

### User Management
Full control over your community.

- **User Directory** — Search and browse all registered members with real-time filtering.
- **Role Assignment** — Assign roles: Member, Admin, Teacher, or Head Admin.
- **Additional Roles** — Toggle special designations: Partner, Resident, Founder, Speaker.
- **Password Management** — Reset user passwords when needed.
- **Create Users** — Manually create accounts for new members.

### Event Management
Create and manage professional events with ease.

- **Create & Edit Events** — Build events with titles, descriptions, dates, locations, capacity limits, and cover images.
- **Draft/Publish Workflow** — Prepare events in draft mode and publish when ready.
- **Custom Registration Forms** — Design event-specific registration forms with a drag-and-drop form builder. Add text fields, dropdowns, checkboxes, file uploads, and more.
- **Registration Management** — View all registrations, filter by status, and manage attendee lists.
- **Attendance Tracking** — Mark attendance manually or use the built-in QR code scanner for fast check-in.
- **QR Code Scanner** — Scan attendee ticket QR codes from a mobile device to instantly record attendance.
- **Featured Events** — Highlight key events on the dashboard and landing page.
- **Registration Status Control** — Open or close registration independently from event publication.
- **Email Attendees** — Send rich-text emails directly to event registrants with a built-in composer.
- **Export to Excel** — Download registration data, attendance records, and event statistics as Excel spreadsheets.

### Reporting & Analytics
Data-driven insights into your club's activity.

- **Event Statistics** — View registration counts, attendance rates, and FU student breakdowns.
- **Comprehensive Reports** — Generate detailed reports across all events with filters and date ranges.
- **Excel Export** — Export any report as a professionally formatted Excel spreadsheet for offline analysis or sharing.
- **Registration Analytics** — Track registration trends, demographics, and custom form responses.

### Course Management (LMS Admin)
Build and deliver online education.

- **Create Courses** — Set up courses with descriptions, module counts, and duration estimates.
- **Module & Lesson Editor** — Organize content into modules and create lessons with rich text and video.
- **Quiz Builder** — Create quizzes with multiple question types to assess student understanding.
- **Assignment Management** — Create assignments, set deadlines, and review student submissions.
- **Grading System** — Grade assignments using customizable rubrics and grade categories.
- **Gradebook** — View and export comprehensive grade reports for any course.
- **Announcements** — Post course-wide announcements to keep students informed.
- **Forum Moderation** — Manage course discussion forums and moderate threads.

### Video & Livestream Management
Curate your media library.

- **Upload Videos** — Add videos with titles and thumbnails, stored securely in cloud storage.
- **Manage Livestreams** — Create, schedule, and manage live broadcasts with RuTube integration.
- **Delete & Organize** — Keep your library clean and up to date.

### Challenge Management
Create engaging AI debate experiences.

- **Create Challenges** — Design debate topics with descriptions, difficulty levels, and time limits.
- **Attach Materials** — Upload reference documents and images for challengers to study.
- **Custom Thumbnails** — Add visual thumbnails to make challenges more appealing.
- **View Results** — Monitor participant performance and leaderboard standings.

### Email Service
Communicate with your community directly from the platform.

- **Rich-Text Email Composer** — Write beautifully formatted emails with a built-in editor.
- **Targeted Sending** — Email specific user groups: event registrants, all members, or selected individuals.
- **HTML Sanitization** — All emails are sanitized for security while preserving formatting.
- **SendGrid Integration** — Reliable email delivery powered by SendGrid's infrastructure.

### Career Portal Administration
Manage job opportunities for your community.

- **Create Job Openings** — Post positions with rich-text descriptions, requirements, and details.
- **Draft/Open/Closed Workflow** — Control when job postings are visible to the public.
- **Application Review** — View and manage all applications with status tracking (new, reviewing, interview, offered, rejected, hired).
- **Candidate Communication** — Message candidates directly about their applications.
- **Resume Access** — View uploaded PDF resumes from applicants.

## Startup & Innovation Platform

A comprehensive innovation ecosystem connecting founders, startups, corporations, and investors. Eight integrated feature areas cover the full journey from idea to scale.

### Startup Profiles
Create and manage your startup's presence on the platform.

- **Create a Startup** — Register your venture with name, description, stage, vertical, and founding year.
- **Team Management** — Invite team members with specific roles: Founder, Co-Founder, Team Member, or Advisor.
- **Metrics Tracking** — Log key business metrics over time: MRR, active users, revenue, and pilot counts.
- **Stage & Vertical** — Classify startups by stage (idea, MVP, growth, scale) and vertical (fintech, edtech, healthtech, etc.).
- **Public Profiles** — Each startup has a browsable public profile with team, description, and key stats.
- **My Startups** — Founders and team members see all startups they belong to in a dedicated tab.

### Corporate Innovation
Dedicated workspace for corporations to manage their innovation activities.

- **Company Workspace** — Dashboard with KPIs, scouting activity, and pipeline overview.
- **Company Profiles** — Create and manage corporate accounts with industry, size, and description.
- **Team Roles** — Invite corporate users as Admin, Scout, or Viewer.
- **Internal Notes** — Keep private notes on scouting progress, meetings, and strategy.
- **Public Employer Branding** — Public company profiles linking programs and job openings for talent attraction.

### Scouting Briefs
Corporations publish structured requests to discover and engage startups.

- **Create Briefs** — Define scouting requirements with title, description, verticals, budget range, and deadlines.
- **Brief Applications** — Startups apply to briefs with fit descriptions and use cases.
- **Application Status** — Track applications through stages: pending, shortlisted, interviewing, selected, or rejected.
- **Brief Management** — Corporations review, shortlist, and manage all applications from a central dashboard.

### Accelerator Programs
Manage multi-format innovation programs with participant tracking.

- **Program Types** — Support for accelerators, incubators, hackathons, workshops, and mentorship programs.
- **Participant Management** — Enroll startups, track status (applied, accepted, active, graduated, dropped), and manage cohorts.
- **Program Details** — Define start/end dates, capacity limits, descriptions, and application criteria.
- **Status Tracking** — Monitor participant progress from application through graduation.

### Evaluation & Scoring
Structured multi-criteria evaluation system for assessing startups.

- **Six Evaluation Criteria** — Score startups on Team, Product, Market, Traction, Strategic Fit, and Risk (0-10 scale).
- **Weighted Scoring** — Each criterion contributes to a calculated overall score.
- **Recommendations** — Evaluators assign a recommendation: Strong Pass, Pass, Consider, or Strong Consider.
- **Detailed Notes** — Add free-text notes for each evaluation for deeper context.
- **History** — Track all evaluations over time for each startup.

### Corporate Pipeline CRM
Kanban-style pipeline for tracking startup-corporate relationships.

- **Pipeline Stages** — Move startups through stages: Discovered, In Evaluation, In Pilot, In Scale-Up, or Archived.
- **Relationship Tracking** — Record how and when a corporate first engaged with a startup.
- **Stage Management** — Drag or move startups between pipeline stages as the relationship evolves.
- **Notes & Context** — Attach notes and context to each pipeline relationship.

### Corporate Reporting
Aggregated KPI dashboards for corporate innovation activity.

- **Dashboard KPIs** — At-a-glance metrics: total briefs, applications received, program counts, pipeline breakdown.
- **Application Analytics** — Track application trends, conversion rates, and sourcing effectiveness.
- **Evaluation Summaries** — Average scores across criteria, recommendation distributions, and evaluator activity.
- **Program Metrics** — Participant counts, graduation rates, and program performance over time.

### Talent Layer
Integration between the innovation ecosystem and the career portal.

- **Startup Hiring** — Startups can post job openings and attract candidates through the career portal.
- **Corporate Jobs** — Corporate partners link their job openings to their company profiles.
- **Employer Branding Pages** — Public company profiles showcase open positions, active programs, and company culture.

## Technical Highlights

- **Secure Authentication** — Dual login options: OAuth or traditional email/password with bcrypt encryption.
- **Role-Based Access Control** — Four-tier permission system (Member, Admin, Teacher, Head Admin) with granular route-level enforcement.
- **Cloud Storage** — All files, images, and documents are stored in Google Cloud Storage via S3-compatible object storage for reliability and speed.
- **Responsive Design** — Fully responsive across desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.
- **Circular Navigation Menu** — A unique radial menu system provides quick, intuitive access to all major sections.
- **SEO Optimized** — Every page includes proper meta tags, Open Graph tags, and structured data for maximum discoverability.
- **Real-Time Updates** — TanStack Query powers instant data synchronization across the platform.
- **Smooth Animations** — Framer Motion delivers polished page transitions, parallax effects, and micro-interactions throughout the interface.
- **GigaChat AI Integration** — Sber's Russian AI powers both the debate challenge system and the conversational event registration chatbot.
